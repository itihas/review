General Ctrl+Tab: Switch from one tab to the next Meeting Alt: Toggle the Always show meeting controls option in General settings Alt+V: Start/stop video Alt+A: Mute/unmute my audio Alt+M: Mute/unmute audio for everyone except the host (available only to the host) Alt+S: Start/stop screen sharing Alt+T: Pause/resume screen sharing Alt+R: Start/stop local recording Alt+C: Start/stop cloud recording Alt+P: Pause/resume recording Alt+N: Switch camera Esc: Enter/exit full screen mode Alt+U: Show/hide participants panel Alt+I: Open the invite window Alt+Y: Raise/lower hand Alt+Shift+R: Begin remote control Alt+Shift+G: Revoke remote control permission Chat Alt+Shift+T: Screenshot Ctrl+W: Close current chat session Phone Ctrl+Shift+A: Accept inbound call. Ctrl+Shift+E: End current call. Ctrl+Shift+D: Decline inbound call. Ctrl+Shift+M: Mute/unmute mic. Ctrl+Shift+H: Hold/unhold call.
