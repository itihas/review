This is the public side of a digital garden, written in org-mode and published using org's native publishing system (source for my configuration). It remains under construction. I split my gardening time between landscaping – getting this site functional and beautiful – and plant care – actually revisiting the mountains of unfinished trains of thought that have accumulated in here over the years.

  • Featured notes are things I'm currently proud of.
  • For interesting entry points look at the links to recently-reviewed pages below. What I review when is driven a great deal by Toricelli (source), a tool that builds a feed out of a garden.
