Org-Notebook

This is the recently-opened public side of a digital garden, bootstrapped from an example braindump included in uniorg. It is deeply under construction. I split my gardening time between landscaping - getting this site functional and less ugly - and plant care - actually revisiting the mountains of unfinished trains of thought that have accumulated in here over the years. We're skewing towards plant care at the moment.

For interesting entry points look at the links below. The list is generated by Toricelli, a tool for surfacing things in a hyperlinked garden to re-interact with. Source here. Yell at me to explain it better in the issue tracker if you feel so inclined.

Top-level navigation is best accomplished via /archive, an exhaustive listing of pages currently exported.

Look for landscaping notes in blogging project.
