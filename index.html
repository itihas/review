Review

This is the recently-opened public side of a digital garden, written in org-mode and published using org's native publishing system (source for my configuration). It is deeply under construction. I split my gardening time between landscaping - getting this site functional and less ugly - and plant care - actually revisiting the mountains of unfinished trains of thought that have accumulated in here over the years.

  • For interesting entry points look at the links to recently-reviewed pages below. What I review when is driven a great deal by Toricelli (source), a tool for surfacing things in a hyperlinked garden to re-interact with. Yell at me in the issue tracker if you want a better explanation than what's in the note so far.
  • Go to /archive.html for a sitemap.
  • Lots of links are broken. This is probably because they link to nodes that aren't in my public folder yet.
