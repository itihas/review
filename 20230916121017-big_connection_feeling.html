big connection feeling

Shallow: the feeling that EVERYTHING IS CONNECTED and IT ALL MAKES SENSE. Very head-y. Associated with / a precursor to frenzy.

Deep: The feeling of perceiving something large and slow-moving and MASSIVE. The feeling of holding a lot of scope and detail in attention, and being aware that the part of it in attention is a very small fraction of the whole. More of a body feeling. Like a deep bass note.
