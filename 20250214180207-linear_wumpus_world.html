linear wumpus world

Wumpus world on a Cartesian coordinate plane \bboldR2\bbold{R}^2\bboldR2. The Wumpus can move to any point. Illustrates the challlenges of too-complex state spaces, how agency is more to do with agent reasoning bounds, goal selection, and the feedback loop between world-learning and goal-writing.
