inside zed's mind

Tags
    :writing:

let me show you what it's like being inside my brain. you will find yourself travelling, and not know where you came from or how you got here; everywhere you turn will be a fresh mystery, and in front of you will always be a new disaster. You will understand nothing, or very little; and all headway is contingent, likely at any moment to be erased by some new development. Survive for a week, and I may begin to listen to you when you tell me what to do or how to feel. I wouldn't trust an engineer that thought they didn't need to look at an engine to fix it, after all. Or a scientist that thought they didn't need to observe things firsthand.
