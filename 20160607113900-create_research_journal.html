Create research journal.

Datetree-style journal, because better organization than chronological happens in snippets and results in a mutable unsavory mess if I try to do it without acknowledging the changing nature of my motivations, interests, ideas, knowledge, and pretty much every other ordering schema I can think of at the moment. A datetree-style journal at least gives me one consolidated file with a consistent organizational structure to read over. It also gives me a good way to move the needle.
